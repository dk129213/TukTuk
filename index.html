Beyond the city walls

Explore the city in a fun, relaxed, and unforgettable way with our Tuk Tuk Tour. Sit back, enjoy the fresh air, and let us take you to the most beautiful viewpoints and hidden corners without the long walks. Our friendly local guide shares stories, history, and insider tips while you cruise comfortably through stunning scenery. From panoramic bridges to breathtaking hilltop views, every stop is a perfect photo opportunity. Whether you choose a daytime ride or a magical sunset tour, you’ll experience the city at your own pace. Easy, personal, and full of great vibes this is sightseeing made simple and memorable.

Signature experiences

120 min

Sightseeing tour of Dubrovnik

Discover Dubrovnik from a unique perspective on a comfortable Tuk Tuk tour that blends history, nature, and breathtaking views. Starting with a scenic drive across the Franjo Tuđman Bridge, you’ll enjoy beautiful sights of the Adriatic and nearby islands. As we continue, enjoy the striking landscape of Mount Srđ, where panoramic views reveal the Old Town, Lokrum Island, and the sparkling coastline below. With fresh air, open views, and plenty of photo stops, this tour offers a perfect balance of sightseeing and comfort. A great choice for visitors who want memorable views without long walks.

120 min

The Golden Hour by tuk tuk

Hop on a Tuk Tuk and explore Dubrovnik’s most beautiful viewpoints in a fun and relaxed way. This expertly timed tour captures the splendor of the Sun sailing beyond the horizon. Taking you across the Franjo Tuđman Bridge, offering stunning views of the Adriatic Sea and surrounding islands. As the Sun goes down, the ride continues up toward Mount Srđ, where a marvelous panoramic view awaits just as the sky lights up in sunset tones. This easy and comfortable experience is perfect for photographers, first-time visitors, and anyone looking to enjoy Dubrovnik’s iconic golden hour without the crowds.

Dubrovnik tale by tuk tuk

Tale of Dubrovnik is a must-do Tuk Tuk tour led by a knowledgeable local guide who brings the city’s views and history to life. Comfortable, authentic, and perfectly paced, this tour combines panoramic drives over the Franjo Tudman Bridge with stunning viewpoints from Mount Srd. The journey finishes in the Old Town, where centuries of history, legends, and local stories are revealed by someone who truly knows the city. With unforgettable views and insider knowledge, Tale of Dubrovnik is the perfect introduction to Dubrovnik.
